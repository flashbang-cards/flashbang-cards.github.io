effective study methods

here are some scientific methods to study effectively (which are my favorites)

01

active recall

testing yourself actively instead of just re-reading. flashcards and practice questions force your brain to retrieve information, strengthening memory pathways.

02

elaborative interrogation

constantly asking "why" and "how" about everything you learn. understand why things happen, not just that they happen.

03

dual coding

combine text with visuals, audio, or hands-on activities. your brain has multiple pathways for storing information.

04

past papers

do lots of exam questions and practice tests. mark yourself extremely harshly - be your own worst critic.

05

spaced repetition

review material multiple times over increasing intervals. revisit topics after 1 day, then 3 days, then a week, then a month.
